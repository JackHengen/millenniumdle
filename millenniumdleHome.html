How to play

Try to guess a mystery Millennium teacher chosen at random

  1. Input any teacher and press guess
  2. The game will tell you whether certain traits of that teacher match the mystery teachers
  3. Using these hints try to guess the mystery teacher in the fewest guesses as possible!
